## ğŸ› ï¸ Production Phases

### **Pre-Alpha**

### **Alpha 1.0**

### **Late Alpha**
- Feature-complete and fun.

### **Beta**
- Sent to QA for testing.
- Major bug fixing and polish.

### **Gold**
- Final release candidate.
- Uploaded to storefronts.
- Day-one patches and updates prepared.
